About

Purpose:

To provide easy access to clinical practice guidelines to facilitate evidence based practice.

Inclusion Criteria:

Clinical Practice Guidelines published after 2010, evaluating therapeutic intervention within the scope of an Australian Physiotherapist.

Consider

Specificity matters: does the therapy mimic the goal?

Intensity matters: is the training sufficiently challenging but not too frustrating?

Repetition matters: can training be set-up in a way that allows the person to practice outside of sessions?

Salience matters: is the training too abstract or does it make sense?

Timing matters: a walking speed of 1.2-1.4m/s (pedestrian crossing) is 4.32 - 5.04kmph on the treadmill
